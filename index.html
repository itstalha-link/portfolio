My Journey

My journey as a creative storyteller has been one of continuous growth and evolution. Over the past three years, I have honed my craft in video editing—transforming raw footage into compelling narratives through expert motion graphics, psychological scriptwriting, and immersive sound design.

As the founder and CEO of Orvion Edge, I lead a dynamic team dedicated to delivering impactful digital solutions. While our agency supports clients with web development, design, and SEO, my true passion lies in crafting videos that resonate deeply, bridging emotion and psychology to create unforgettable stories.

Balancing my role as a BBA student with running a creative agency, I am committed to "crafting a new world of storytelling"—where every frame, sound, and script is thoughtfully designed to connect and inspire.
