About Me

I’m a storyteller at heart—blending artistry and smart thinking to craft videos that don’t just look great, but work harder for your brand.

My Journey

"Your footage is just raw clay. I sculpt stories."

My love affair with storytelling began in the glow of gaming montages, where every cut had to feel right. But I craved more—more truth, more depth. That hunger led me to 3D documentaries, where I learned to weave realism with raw emotion. Now? I craft corporate stories that don't just play—they pulse.

For 3 years, I've obsessed over the alchemy of motion graphics, sound design, and psychological hooks—because a great video isn't watched; it's felt. (And yes, my late-night book binges—from Jung to Pixar's storytelling rules—fuel every script.)

As CEO of Orvion Edge, I bridge boardrooms and heartbeats: websites that convert, videos that haunt memories. But my true north? Making 'professional' feel human.

(Bonus: I pull this off between BBA lectures. Sleep is overrated when there are stories to tell.)

Let's not just create—let's ignite.
